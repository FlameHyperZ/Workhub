Groak


Groak – while the origin of this word is unknown, it means to watch someone silently
as they eat, in the hope that you will be invited to join them. For instance, how am I supposed
to enjoy my sushi while that guy is groaking me the entire time? Whoa, that sounds similar to
something else…maybe let’s not revive that one…
